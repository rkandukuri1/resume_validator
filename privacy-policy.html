Privacy Policy

AI Resume Validator

AI Resume Validator respects your privacy. This extension is designed to help users validate resumes against job descriptions and generate improved resume content using AI.

Information We Process

We may temporarily process the following information:

  • Resume text uploaded by the user
  • Job description text entered by the user

How We Use Information

The information is used only to:

  • Analyze resume compatibility with job requirements
  • Identify missing skills
  • Generate improved resume content

Data Storage

We do not permanently store resumes, job descriptions, or generated results. Data is processed temporarily through our secure backend service.

Third-Party Services

This extension uses secure AI services provided by OpenAI to process resume content and generate responses.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Security

We use secure communication between the extension and backend services to protect submitted data.
